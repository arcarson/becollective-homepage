It's all about

Doing what we can, when we can

Be Collective is your digital gateway to helping your community and realising your positive social impact. Find volunteer opportunities, join groups, run events, manage people and engage new audiences.
Be Collective has something for everyone.

Give back to your community
and watch your social record grow.

A verified social record that recognises your contribution

And creates better pathways to employment

Be Collective is a platform for action and a tool that asks what you can do - not what you can't, and highlights what has been achieved - not what hasn't.
Be Collective is for everybody and there are no barriers to starting your journey and having your efforts recorded, verified and celebrated.
A personalised, intuitive experience that makes volunteering more meaningful.
